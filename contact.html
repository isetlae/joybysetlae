About Joy in A Box

Joy in A Box is a home décor brand based in Maseru, Lesotho. We started with a simple goal — to bring beautiful, affordable, and quality home products to every household. From soft snuggle items to custom-made décor pieces, everything we make is crafted with love and care.


We believe that a beautiful home brings joy to everyone in it. That is why we work hard to offer you products that are both stylish and affordable.
