Made-To-Order

We create custom pieces just for you — your style, your choice.

How It Works

Step 1 – Contact Us

Reach out to us via WhatsApp, email, or phone and tell us what you need.

Step 2 – Share Your Ideas

Send us your design ideas, colour preferences, or reference images.

Step 3 – We Make a Quote

We will send you a price quote based on your requirements.

Step 4 – Confirm & Pay Deposit

Once you approve the quote, pay a 50% deposit to begin production.

Step 5 – We Create Your Item

Our team crafts your custom piece with care and attention to detail.

Step 6 – Delivery or Collection

Your item is delivered to your door or ready for collection in Maseru.

What We Can Make For You

  • Custom cushion covers with your chosen fabric
  • Personalised wall frames and prints
  • Hand-painted plant pots
  • Handmade gift boxes and hampers
  • Custom table runners and placemats
  • Matching bedroom décor sets

Turnaround Time

  1. Simple items: 3 – 5 working days
  2. Medium projects: 7 – 10 working days
  3. Large / complex projects: 2 – 3 weeks

* Delivery times may vary during busy periods. We will always keep you updated.
